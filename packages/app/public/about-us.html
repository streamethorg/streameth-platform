    Our mission

    StreamETH was born in 2022 as a decentralized alternative to traditional streaming platforms to distribute Devconnect Amsterdam to the online crypto community.

    Since then, StreamETH developed to be the leading tool for hybrid/ virtual event distribution in the Ethereum space developing features like LiveNFTs minting and unlocking new monetization primitives for event organizers. The team is composed by a group of professionals with extensive experience in event production and media management in both Web2 and Web3.
